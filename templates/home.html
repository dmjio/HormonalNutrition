Kevin Kuhn

Kevin Kuhn, M.S.Ed., CSCS is a Nutrition Coach, Kinesiologist, and Certified Strength and Conditioning Specialist. He earned a s degree in Exercise Science from Cedarville University, and a s degree in Exercise Physiology from Baylor University. Kevin has trained and provided nutritional coaching for clients running the gambit of the population. He has coached pre-adolescent and adolescent athletes, collegiate and professional athletes, individuals seeking to change their weight and body composition, as well as elderly populations. He has previously worked as the head strength and conditioning coach and nutrition coach for the Indiana Invaders professional running club, and now serves the team as a consulting coach. Kevin currently serves as a Nutrition and Athletic Performance Coach at Modern Fitness, located in Dallas/Farmersville, Texas. Kevin currently lives in the great state of Texas and plans on beginning work towards a PhD in the fall of 2013.
